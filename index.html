A Letter for Sara 💌

🌸

June 17th

My dearest Sara Kutty,

I've been thinking about what to write for days now. And honestly? I could fill a thousand pages and still not do justice to what you mean to me. So I'll just try.

You are the most delightfully confusing person I have ever met. One minute you're making the most ridiculous face I've ever seen on a human being — Monkey mode, fully activated — and the next minute you're saying something so thoughtful it actually stops me in my tracks. That's the Sara paradox. You are chaos and calm at the same time, and somehow it works perfectly.

Bubbu, you turned 23 today. And I think you should know that this year, every year, you have managed to become more yourself. More confident. More certain of what you want. You flex those biceps like you built them specifically to remind the world that you are not to be underestimated — and honestly? You're right. You never should be.

I love that you love football the way you do. I love that you have opinions and you argue them loudly and you feel things deeply when your team wins or loses. That competitive fire in you is one of the things I admire most — it shows up in everything you do, not just on the pitch.

And then there are flowers. Hand you a bouquet and something shifts — your whole face softens, your eyes light up, and for a moment the chaos disappears and it's just Sara, happy and present and glowing. I want to give you flowers forever just to keep seeing that face.

I think about the Monkey in you often. The goofy faces, the random sounds, the way you make even the most ordinary afternoon feel like a memory worth keeping. You don't try to be funny — you just are. And I am so, so grateful for every ridiculous moment.

But mostly, Sara, I want you to know this: you have made my world softer and brighter and so much more full. Being with you feels like choosing the right song for exactly the right moment. You fit. You always fit.

23 is yours. Take it fully, loudly, beautifully — the way only you can.

With everything I have,

Your person 💕
